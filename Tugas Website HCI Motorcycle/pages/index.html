YAMADA MT-15 New Color?

The latest Yamada MT-15 slides with a new color choice in Malaysia. In line with these changes, the price of this naked sport motorbike has increased compared to previous model. The official MT-15 as of June has reached 12,298 ringgit, equivalent to Rp. 40.9 million. Meanwhile the price for the 2021 MT-15 is set at 11,988 ringgit or the equivalent of Rp. 39.9 million.
